Who We Are

We don't just build.
We conspire with context.

CAFCA stands for Constructive AF Collaborative Architecture. Our ideology is simple: ego kills design. We embrace the collective voice of the client, the mason, and the architect.

**Materiality:** We strip back the superficial. We favor raw concrete, locally sourced timber, and exposed brick. Earthy tones aren't a choice; they are the default state of our materials.
